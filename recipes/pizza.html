Pizza Recipe

Description:

A classic thin-crust Italian pizza loaded with spicy tomato sauce, smoky pepperoni or salami, and a generous kick of crushed red chili flakes. Finished with a drizzle of chili oil, it's simple, fiery, and authentically Italian.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ball pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup crushed San Marzano tomatoes (or good quality tomato purée)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil (plus extra for drizzling)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1–2 tsp crushed red chili flakes (adjust to heat preference)
  • 1/2cups shredded mozzarella cheese (low-moisture preferred)
  • 8–10 slices spicy salami or pepperoni/li>
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)
  • Pinch of salt

Steps to Prepare:

  1. Preheat your oven to its highest setting (250°C / 480°F) with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside for at least 30 minutes
  2. Make the sauce: In a small bowl, mix the crushed tomatoes, minced garlic, olive oil, oregano, 1 teaspoon of chili flakes, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Stretch the dough on a floured surface into a 12-inch round circle and transfer it to a piece of parchment paper.
  4. Spread the sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border for the crust.
  5. Bake for 8–12 minutes until the crust is golden and bubbly and the cheese is melted with charred spots.
  6. Remove from the oven, sprinkle with the remaining chili flakes, drizzle with a little extra olive oil, and garnish with fresh basil. Slice and serve hot!
